All activities within the scope of this study were conducted in the context of the HAT passive surveillance program implemented in Burkina Faso by the Neglected Tropical Diseases National Control Program with the support of the WHO and the UMR Intertryp IRD-CIRAD/CIRDES (Bobo-Dioulasso), which is a WHO collaborative centre. We acknowledge all the technicians from the HAT teams of CIRDES and all health professionals involved in this HAT passive surveillance system in Burkina Faso.
