Severe fever with thrombocytopenia syndrome is a serious natural focal disease caused by SFTS virus. At present, it has become one of the main public health problems in Jinan City. Although some epidemiological surveys have been conducted, there is a lack of relevant spatiotemporal statistical research and the latest information. This study conducts statistical analysis of the latest data in the past 5 years to grasp the spatiotemporal clustering characteristics and change trends of SFTS in Jinan City, analyze the geographical environment and social characteristics of hot areas, then to allocate health resources more reasonably, and provide a basis for precise prevention and control of SFTS.
