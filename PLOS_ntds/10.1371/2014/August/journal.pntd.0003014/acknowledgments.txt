The authors acknowledge the Secretary for Health and Child Care and Permanent Secretary for Education Sport, Arts and Culture for their support and provision of human resources during the implementation of the national survey. Provincial Medical Directors, Provincial Education Directors are acknowledged for the provision of transport, human resources and laboratory space during the national survey. The Medical laboratory scientists from the National Institute of Health Research and those drawn from the provinces who participated in field data collection are thanked. The District Education Officers are thanked for their assistance in locating schools selected for the national survey in their districts and for facilitating travelling to schools by the research teams. Finally, Professor Anthony Butterworth is acknowledged for the advice during the design of the national survey.
